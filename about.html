Meet Alison Sommer

Originally from Columbus, Ohio, I am a current Sophomore studying Graphic Design at West Virginia University in Morgantown, WV. When I am not working on projects or artwork, you can find me running, getting coffee, watching movies, or spending time with friends and family.

In my work, I strive to create meaningful and intentional work. My goal as both a student and artist is to work towards enhancing my skills and experiences so my designs can better communities.

This app is my start at creating work that improves daily lives and experiences through UI design.
